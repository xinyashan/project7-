Visual novel game

  • Your path! Your choice!
  • Be good! or be evil!

You are a witch in training who has recently just finished training from the guild! You have been chosen to fight the demons in those dark gloomy dungeons and also have the chance to earn some gold while sweeping the demons out of this world! Your other tasks also include completeing quests requested by citizens who need your aid! Fight through the eerie dungeons, explore the town, rack up on gold and buy some things from the magic shop to upgrade your character and continue to level up your character throughout the game!
